The outside is part of the stay.

A private garden for marshmallows, BBQs and muddy boots.

French doors open to a large fenced garden on a private plot, surrounded by greenery. There is firewood ready, outdoor candles, warm plaids, bamboo sticks for marshmallows, a gas BBQ and a fire-pit tripod for cooking outside.

What makes it special

Wooded charm, polder quiet.

Walks from the door

The protected bird breeding area begins in front of the house on the dike — best at sunrise or sunset.

Bikes waiting

Cycle through forests, polders and wide heaths; restaurants in Laren and Blaricum are within cycling distance.

Cosy in every season

Fireplaces, candles, blankets and plenty of firewood make winter stays feel especially warm.

Private parking

Your own parking space on the plot, with a lit path to the guest entrance.

Near everything

Where you are

Between farms, forests and good food.

Eemnes sits between Laren and Blaricum in the heart of the Gooi: quiet enough for birdsong, close enough for restaurants, shopping and day trips.

On footSupermarkets, bakery, greengrocer and dike walks in Eemnes.
By bikeLaren and Blaricum restaurants, forests, polders and open heaths.
